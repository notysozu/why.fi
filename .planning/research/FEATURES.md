# Features Research: Meme Expression Analyzer

## Table Stakes (Must Have)
- **Live Video Feed**: Low latency and un-lagged webcam access.
- **Real-time Processing**: Sub-100ms processing loop from camera to server and back to UI.
- **Face & Emotion Detection**: Core tracking mechanism to analyze user states.
- **Meme & Audio Reactions**: Instant audiovisual feedback triggered by backend metrics.

## Differentiators (Competitive Advantage)
- **Gamified "Boss Fight" UI**: Health bars, chaos metrics (the "Why Meter"), and progressive difficulty.
- **Hostile UX ("The Trap")**: Keyboard locking and warning screens when trying to leave the page or tab.
- **Emoji Mimicry Challenge**: Active "escape" mechanism requiring physical performance from the user.

## Anti-Features (What deliberately NOT to handle)
- **Static Image Support**: Weakens the live interactive trap feeling.
- **User Accounts/Auth**: Destroys the immediate, frictionless meme accessibility.
- **Data Persistence (beyond the session)**: Keep it stateless to avoid privacy/GDPR complexities on funny facial data.
